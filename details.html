About the Venue

The Villas of Grand Cypress is a resort wedding venue located in Orlando, Florida. This highly-rated Orlando family golf resort features idyllic ceremony locations, elegant reception venues, and unrivaled service to make our day nothing short of spectacular. Revel in The Villas of Grand Cypress’s natural beauty and luxurious setting; you’ll find the planning, décor, cuisine and services are all exquisite at this breathtaking venue.

Rooms, Features, and Amenities
The Villas of Grand Cypress is an oasis in the midst of the Orlando fun, offering lavish accommodations with all the comforts of home. Enjoy oversized accommodations with spacious suites and villas of up to four bedrooms with full kitchens, living and dining areas. All rooms are within walking distance to the resort pool and sauna. Resort activities include 45 holes at the Jack Nicklaus signature designed golf course, golf instruction, a fitness center, swimming pool, sauna, hot tub, complimentary bicycles, nature trails, fishing poles and tackle, and in-room spa services.

Location
The Villas of Grand Cypress is located just minutes away from Orlando’s famed theme parks and attractions. They offer complimentary transportation to Walt Disney World, Universal Orlando and SeaWorld Orlando. They also offer a shuttle to nearby attractions, such as the Marilyn Monroe Spa, Pitch ‘n Putt Golf, Rock Wall, Lake Windsong water sports, Game Room and additional dining options.
