Our Story

How we Met
It all began in 2009 at a mutual friend’s going away party. Hunter learned about love at first sight that night. He couldn’t take his eyes off of Megan. Megan caught Hunter looking at her from across the room. She approached him and introduced herself. Little did she know that moment was the beginning of a beautiful romance.

Our Story
Hunter and Megan dated for a few months before moving to Orlando to attend UCF together. Once Hunter graduated he moved to Colorado while Megan stayed in Orlando. After a year of their long-distance relationship Megan planned to move to join Hunter in Colorado. She jumped on a plane to finalize her new living arrangements with Hunter in Colorado. On the drive back to the Denver airport from her visit it was snowing heavily, and she couldn’t stop thinking about how far her family would be. Hunter reassured her that it would all be okay. Upon her arrival in Orlando she received a surprising text from Hunter who explained he had quit his job and would be moving home to Orlando instead.
They did not stay long in Orlando though. Hunter got a job offer in Atlanta. They packed up the house and their puppy, and set off on another adventure to explore a new city. Atlanta brought great new experiences and friends. Three quick years later Megan got an offer to join a Jacksonville-based company. Once again, these two love birds shipped off to began a new chapter in their current home of Jacksonville.

The Proposal
In March 2017, Hunter and Megan set off on a romantic vacation in Ireland. Megan had some suspicions that Hunter may propose. However, as they traversed the Emerald Isle she began to doubt it would happen. Then on the 11th day of March, the two of them set off on an adventure to hike to the top of Slieve League - some the highest sea cliffs in Europe.
As they journeyed upward - Hunter slightly up ahead and Megan beginning to loose hope - they finally ascended to a gorgeous outlook. A picture must be had. Hunter pulled out his tripod and the two of them snapped away with the breathtaking Atlantic ocean to their backs. Then, with a tremble in his voice, Hunter kneeled down and uttered the most precious of words, "will you be my wife"? To which Megan, with teary eyes, exclaimed, "yes"!
